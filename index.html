About Me

I am currently pursuing my M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UC Davis. My academic and project experience has cultivated a strong foundation in CMOS analog circuit design, VLSI, and integrated circuits tapeout.

Through detailed coursework and diverse collaborative projects, I've harnessed skills across Cadence Virtuoso, HSPICE, Python, and PCB design. From designing transistor-level CMOS inverters to engineering hardware prototypes for bidirectional DC-DC converters, my passion lies in creating efficient hardware solutions.

Outside the lab, I'm actively involved in research on deep-sea mining systems and solar-integrated microgrids. I thrive in challenging engineering environments and am always eager to contribute to cutting-edge technology teams.
